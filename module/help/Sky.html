Sky

HDRI Map

Map for stage lighting. Click on the button with three dots (...) and indicate where your HDRI map lies or drag the map from the "Content Browser".

Daylight Object

Field for Daylight object. Align your sun with the sun spot on the HDRI map, place the Daylight object in the Daylight Object field and rotate your map along with the sun through the "Rotation HDRI" slider in the Sky object's settings. ATTENTION! Before combining the visible points, zero the "Rotation HDRI" slider. To move the sun on the map, use the "Rotation X / Rotation Y" sliders in the Daylight object settings.

Show Preview / Radius Preview

Show an object that displays the position of your HDRI map.

Rotation HDRI

Rotate the HDRI map. Rotation occurs only along the X axis.

Sky Brightness

The brightness of your HDRI map.

Hue / Saturation

The parameter is responsible for the hue and saturation of the HDRI map.

Lightness

The parameter is responsible for how much the HDRI map needs to be highlighted or darkened.

Colorize

Tinting in one color, is used together with sliders "Hue / Saturation".

Brightness

Adds all colors to the saturation.

Contrast

Contrast HDRI map.

Gamma

Gamma HDRI map.
